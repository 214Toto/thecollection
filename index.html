MBS 200 - Human Anatomy

★ The course has three Branches being taught by two Lecturers at the moment; Dr. Mulenga & Mrs. D. Chikwanda
★ It is one of the pivots of medicine, especially on the surgery part and you will refering to it as you do other courses like pathology, clicical science and physiology,
★ thus it is very bulky, stuyding in advance is highly recommended, Either you are a 2nd or 3rd year student
★ The Branches of this course are;

  1. Gross Anatomy » This branch deals with what you can see with your naked eye, from the surface anatomy to the deep structures within the body.
  2. Histology » This one is basically about what you cannot see with your naked eyes. Microscopic analysation of different tissues of the body as well as their constituent cells is what you will be doing here
  3. Embryology » You will focus on the development of every body structure right from before fusion of the sperm and the ovum till all the systems of a fetus are fully developed.
