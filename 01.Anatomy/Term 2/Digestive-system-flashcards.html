The DIGESTIVE SYSTEM

The pancreas has two main ducts, namely ?
✓ pancreatic duct
✓ accessory duct
Pancreatic duct joins common bile duct and enters duodenum as the ?
✓ hepatopancreatic ampulla (Ampulla of vater)
What are the other names for the Pancreatic Duct and the Accesory Duct ?
✓ Pancreatic Duct ⇾ duct of Wirsung
✓ Accesory Duct ⇾ duct of Santorini
From the Histological Aspect of the pancreas. WHat portions does it have ?
✓ Exocrine portions => 99% of cells
✓ Endocrine Portions => 1% of cells
What type of cells are in each portion
✓ Acini & pancreatic islets (islets of Langerhans)
List the hormones that the Endocrine portion secretes
✓ glucagon, insulin, somatostatin, and pancreatic polypeptide.
Approximately how much of the Pancreatic Juice is produced
✓ 1200–1500 mL
What does the Pancreatic juice consist of ?
✓ mostly of water, some salts, sodium bicarbonate, and several enzymes
What is the importance of the Sodium Bicarbonate in digestion ?
✓ it gives pancreatic juice a slightly alkaline pH (7.1–8.2) that buffers acidic gastric juice in chyme
✓ stops the action of pepsin from the stomach, and creates the proper pH for the action of digestive enzymes in the small intestine
The enzymes in pancreatic juice include a starch digesting enzyme called ?
✓ pancreatic amylase
What Proteolytic enzymes are found in the Pancreatic Juice
✓ trypsin
✓ chymotrypsin
✓ carboxypeptidase
✓ elastase
