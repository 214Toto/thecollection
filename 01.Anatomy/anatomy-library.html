Cimetidine

Class:

Histamine 2 Receptor Antagonists (H2As)

Mechanism: PIG

  • Blocks Histamine 2 receptors, leading to:
    • Pepsinogen activation failure to pepsin
    • Intrinsic Factor low secretion but not enough to reduce vitamin B12 absorption
    • Gastric Acid secretion prevention

Pharmacokinetics:

  • Absorption: GUT. then undergoes hepatic inactivation b4 secretion thru urine
  • has long duration of action inspite of having a half-life of 2-3hrs
  • given once or twice a day

Indications: PH GAD

  • PUD & Heartburn
  • GERD/GORD & Allergic reactions when used in combination with H1 receptor blockers & Dyspepsia
