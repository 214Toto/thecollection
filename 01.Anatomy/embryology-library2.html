Umbilical Arteries

Description:

Extension of the Dorsal Aortae at its caudal end into the connecting stalk

Which ppt can i find it ?

  • Development of Blood Vessels ppt
  • Page 13

Function:

  • Carries Deoxygenated Blood from the fetal crculation to the Placenta
  • After birth;
    • proximal parts of umbilical arteries form superior vesicle arteries
    • distal parts form fibrous ligaments called medial umbilical ligaments

spiral septum

Description:

structure formed by union of the right superior & left inferior truncus swellings or cushions

Which ppt can i find it ?

  • Development of Blood Vessels ppt......Page 24
