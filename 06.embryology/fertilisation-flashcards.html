When is the secondary oocyte expelled ?
✓At ovulation from the ovarian follicle with the escaping fluid
WHat helps the movement of the ovum upon it being released ?
✓ sweeping action & cilia of the mucosal cells of the fimbriae "sweep" the secondary oocyte into the funnel-shaped infundibulum of the uterine tube
Which part of the uterine tube does the oocyte pause ?
✓ Ampulla
Name all the parts of the uterine tube
✓ Infundibulum
✓ Ampulla
✓ Isthmus
Epididymis is the storage for sperms, what aids the transport of the sperms from there ?
✓ peristaltic contractions of the thick muscular coat of the ductus deferens rapidly transport sperms to the urethra
