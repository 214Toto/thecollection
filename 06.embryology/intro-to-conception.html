Introduction to Conception and Growth (Embryology)

Define Embryology ?
✓ study of how body structures form and develop before birth
What is prenatal Development ?
✓ period of development from fertilization to birth
WHat does the term Post Natal Development refer to ?
✓
Embryologically prenatal period is divided into 3 parts. Namely..?
✓ Pre-embryonic/Preimplanatation Period
✓ Embryonic Period
✓ Fetal Period
Define the length of the Pre-embryonic Period
✓ conception (fertilization) to the end of 2nd week of intrauterine life (IUL)
WHat morphongenic events take place during this period ? The Pre-embryonic Period.
✓ transportation of zygote through the uterine tube
✓ fertilization
✓ mitotic divisions/cleavage
✓ implantation
✓ formation of primordial embryonic tissues
WHat is the span of the embryonic Period ?
✓ beginning of 3rd week to the end of 8th week of Intrauterine life
WHat morphongenic events take place during this period ? The Embryonic Period.
✓ differentiation of 3 germ layers into specific body organs and the formation of placenta
✓ umbilical cord
✓ extraembryonic membranes
When does the fetal period start and end ?
✓ beginning of the 9th week to birth
What events take place during this period ?
✓ tremendous growth
✓ specialization of the body structures
What is the Postnatal Period of development ?
✓ it is the period from birth of an individual to adulthood
List the Divisions of Postnatal Period of development, up to Adulthood, while indicating the respective ages for each.
✓ Neonatal period: birth to 28 days after birth
✓ Infancy:1 month to 1 year
✓ Childhood: 2nd year to 12th year of age
✓ Puberty: 12 years to 16 years
✓ Adolescence: 17 years to 20 years
✓ Adulthood: 21 years to 40 years.
